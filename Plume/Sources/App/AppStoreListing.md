## Full Description

**The reading tracker that actually works.**

Plume is the simplest way to track your physical book reading. Photograph a cover. Tap in your current page. Watch Plume calculate your pace and predict your finish date.

No barcode scanning. No ISBN lookup. No accounts. Just you and your books.

**How it works:**

📖 **Add any book in seconds** — Photograph the cover. Enter the title, author, and total pages. Done.

📈 **Track your pace** — Log the last page you read. Plume calculates your pages-per-day rate automatically.

🔮 **See your finish date** — Based on your reading speed, Plume tells you exactly when you'll close the last page.

📚 **Build your library** — Every book you've ever tracked lives in your personal reading history.

**For serious readers, upgrade to Scholar:**

Export your favorite quotes with professional citation formats (APA, MLA, Chicago). Track your reading streaks. Get personalized book recommendations. All your reading data, beautifully formatted and portable.

**Why Plume?**

- Warm, bookish design that feels like a private library
- No social features, no gamification, no noise
- Works completely offline
- Your reading data stays on your device

*Start your first book today. Finish more of them.*
