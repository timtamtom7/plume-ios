## Tagline

## Editorial / Parchment Aesthetic

PlumeMac channels the warmth of a well-worn journal into a modern writing environment. Every pixel feels intentional — like ink on cream paper, or a fountain pen resting beside an open manuscript.

**Visual language:**
- Warm parchment backgrounds (#F5F0E8 / #FAFAF8)
- Deep ink-blue typography (#1E3A5F)
- Antique gold accents (#C9A227)
- Serif headings (Georgia), clean body text (SF Pro)
- Soft shadows, rounded cards, generous whitespace

## Description (App Store Copy)

**WRITE WITHOUT BOUNDARIES.**

PlumeMac is a distraction-free writing environment crafted for writers who care about the experience as much as the output. Whether you're drafting your next novel, journaling your thoughts, or chasing daily word goals, PlumeMac adapts to your rhythm.

**Built for writers:**
- ✍️ Daily writing prompts to spark inspiration
- 📊 Track your streaks, word counts, and writing habits
- 🎨 A beautiful parchment aesthetic that feels warm and inviting
- 🧠 AI-powered style analysis to sharpen your voice
- 📚 Community prompts — share ideas, discover new perspectives
- 🔥 Writing challenges to keep you accountable

PlumeMac isn't just another text editor. It's a companion for your writing life.
